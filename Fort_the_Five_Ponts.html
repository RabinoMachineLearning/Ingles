Fort the Five Ponts

The Fort of SÃ£o Tiago das Cinco Pontas was the last Dutch construction in Recife and its objectives were to protect the capital of Pernambuco from foreign attacks and help in the distribution of drinking water. During a restoration, the Fort lost one of its points, but the name "five points" continues to be used in the city. Currently, the place houses the City Museum, with temporary exhibitions that tell a little about the history of Recife. Free entrance.
